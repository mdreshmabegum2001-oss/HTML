Welcome to SMVM Polytechnic College.

Sri Mullapudi Venkataraya Memorial Polytechnic.

is a polytechnic college offering diploma courses.

Situated at Tanuku, West Godavari District, Andhra Pradesh, it was established in 1958.

It is one of the oldest polytechnic institutes in India.

Recently, the government of Andhra Pradesh granted SDC (Skill Development Centre) having many training programmes like ProgeCAD and it is also holding the development of Rajiv Yuva Kiranalu programme.
